Frequently asked

Do I need an account?

No. Talkimals works right away — no sign-up, no login, no email required.

Does it work without internet?

Talking to your animal friend runs on your device. An internet connection may be needed the first time to download the voice, and for ads or purchases.

How do I turn off ads?

Subscribe to Talkimals+ (in the app, tap "Unlock with Talkimals+" or open Settings → Get Talkimals+). It removes all ads and unlocks every animal, world, and voice.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

On your device, open Settings → Apple Account → Subscriptions, choose Talkimals, and manage or cancel there. Cancelling stops future renewals; you keep access until the period ends.

I bought Talkimals+ but it isn't unlocked.

Open the app's Settings and tap Restore purchases, or on the upgrade screen tap Restore. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple Account used to buy.

How do I turn notifications on or off?

In the app: Settings → Notifications. You can also manage them in iOS Settings.

How do I delete my data?

Your child's profile and progress are stored only on the device — deleting the app removes them.
